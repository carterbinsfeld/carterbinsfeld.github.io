PACT Speech Team

About this project

One of my earliest client works from high school, this piece was created for my school's Speech Team for use on web and merchandise alike. We went through several iterations, with both the school mascot and team mascot, before settling on an all-caps, bold typeface that combined well with the school logo. This shows off the loud and proud goals of the speech team itself, while emphasizing school spirit through the mascot and school colors being represented.

Year 2019
Client PACT Charter School
