Typographers of the 1900s Booklet

About this project

This project was made for Intro to Typography with Annie Peters in Spring of 2025. The goal for this assignment was to make a concise, short booklet that introduced a few significant type designers throughout modern history and their contributions. I opted for a more retro color palette that felt reminiscent of early Apple and Polaroid, creating a vintage aesthetic. As a unique challenge to myself, I used cutouts for the designer's names, which was an interesting learning curve within Adobe InDesign.
