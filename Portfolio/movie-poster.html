Photoshop Exercise

Satirical Movie Poster

About this project

Created for Intro to Graphic Design with Annie Peters in Fall of 2024, this movie poster was an enjoyable project with a more familiar software to me, being Adobe Photoshop. As I had just begun my formal education with Adobe at this point, I felt I was able to really stretch my legs while creating something that had personality and felt personalized to myself. Featuring my cat, this work is a satirization of the overly-yellowed drama posters of the mid-2010s.
