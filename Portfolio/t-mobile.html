T-Mobile Rebrand

About this project

Created for ART335 Design Bureau in Spring of 2026, this rebranding project aimed tot ackle some struggles T-Mobile ahd in the public eye through a change in their overall look.

Using a bolder, sturdier typeface along with a mong expansive color palette, T-Mobile maintains it's brand recognition while feeling fresh and reliable. The small, accented "period" brings about a sense of confidence and finality, that what you see is what you get, no tricks.

Overall, this project challenged me and expanded my abilites in the research and conceptualization phases of design, as well as pushing me to create a more cohesive and expansive brand identity.
