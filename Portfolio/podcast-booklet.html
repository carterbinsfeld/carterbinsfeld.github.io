About this project

This project was created for Advanced Typography with David Stairs in Fall of 2025. Here my task was to transcribe an episode of a podcast into a readable format. While this alone was a fine task, creating a booklet that felt enjoyable to read and pleasing to look at was another. With this assignment, I wanted to go further than just creating something passable, but something that felt as though it benefitted from its new form factor, so as though the reader wouldn't lose any of the original charm from the podcast creators. This led to prioritizing breathing room in between speakers, and making sure any interjections and pacing were well thought out.
